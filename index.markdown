I am a research engineer in NIST’s Communications Technology Lab. Currently I use reliability analysis techniques to demonstrate and assess the benefits and risks of using closed box tools and technology for the condition monitoring of manufacturing systems. This effort follows the broader picture of facilitating the adoption of Industry of Things-enabled (IoT-enabled) operations and maintenance of manufacturing systems.

I have completed a B.S. degree in Electrical Engineering  (2012), an M.S. degree in Systems Engineering (2014), and a Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering (2025), all at the University of Maryland, College Park.
